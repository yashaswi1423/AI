  • Participants must carry their own laptops.

Rules and Regulations

General Rules

  • Teams must not switch members after the hackathon begins.
  • All team members must be present throughout the hackathon.
  • Team will be disqualified if any Team's Table is found empty without any participant.
  • Plagiarism leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Participants will not be allowed to go out of campus once the hackathon begins.
  • All decisions by the judging panel and organizers are final and binding.

Hackathon Ethics

  • Respect venue/platform rules.
  • Collaborate ethically.
  • No offensive, discriminatory, or illegal content in submissions.

Requirements

  • Participants shall bear any hardware or software requirements related to their project.
  • Participants must carry their own laptops and peripherals.
  • Participants are advised to carry all the necessity for 48 hours (Warmers, Extra Blankets, Chargers, Prescribed Medicines).
  • Participants must carry their valid college ID Cards and Aadhar Card.

Conduct and Discipline

  • Participants must behave cordially and cooperate with other members.
  • Participants must not damage property/facilities and must keep discipline.
  • Participants must adhere to dress code (semi formal attire).
  • Participants must maintain cleanliness of campus.
  • It is the sole responsibility of organizing committee to disqualify participants indulged in misbehaviour of any sort.
  • No TA/DA will be provided to participants.
  • Alcohol, drugs and narcotics or any banned items are strictly prohibited inside the campus.

Facilities & Support

  • Wi-Fi and power available 24x7
  • Food and beverages
  • Place to rest and Sleep
  • Mentors for troubleshooting
  • Emergency Medical support
